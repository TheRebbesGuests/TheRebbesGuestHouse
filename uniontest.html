The Union House

Located just steps from 770, The Union House is one of our most spacious and comfortable guesthouses. It can host up to 25 guests, making it ideal for large families or groups looking to spend Shabbos together in a warm, Chassidish environment.

The house features (TBD bedrooms and bathrooms – please update), a full kosher kitchen, and a finished basement. It’s stocked with linens, towels, and Shabbos essentials like a blech and hot water urn. You’ll also enjoy close proximity to local shuls and shops.

There is also an option to book just the basement unit, which comfortably sleeps up to 4 people. This is a great choice for smaller groups or those looking for a more budget-friendly stay.
